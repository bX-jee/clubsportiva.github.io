Club Sportiva - Member Update December 20th, 2025
 
 

Member Update
 

As we celebrate the season, please note our upcoming holiday schedule:

 

Christmas Eve – Closing at 12:00PM

Christmas Day – Closed

New Year’s Eve – Closing at 12:00PM

New Year’s Day – Closed

 

After the holidays, we’re looking ahead to a fresh start with a lineup of experiences designed to begin the new year feeling energized, refreshed, and well-connected. 

 

We’ll kick things off on January 9th with Bodyspec at Club Sportiva, open to all members from 11:30AM to 6:00PM. This in-depth body composition scan offers valuable insights to help you set informed health goals for the year ahead.

 

The following day, January 10th, all members are invited to our Windshield Repair & Door Ding Service Day from 10:00AM to 2:00PM. It’s the perfect opportunity to start the year with your car looking its best; quick, convenient, and handled right at the Club.

 

Later in the month, we’ll gather on January 22nd from 6:00PM to 9:00PM for Scotch & Cigar Night at the Clubhouse, exclusively for Enthusiast & Executive Members. Expect an evening of refined pours, premium cigars, and great conversation to close out the month in style.

 

From all of us at the Club, we wish you and your loved ones a happy holiday season and a wonderful start to the new year!
